IPUMS website offers variables/characteristics upon
which one can extract datasets of population

Broad Categories of Variables:
	0 Households - broader
	0 Person - more specific

- Unique ID for households:
	Combination of YEAR, DATANUM, SERIAL
	datanum: is sampling method used
	serial: is serial # of household
 
- Unique ID for person:
	Combination of YEAR, DATANUM, SERIAL
	and PERNUM
